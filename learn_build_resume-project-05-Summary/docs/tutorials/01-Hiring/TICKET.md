# Understanding the Hiring Process

## Objective

Learn how American companies hire and understand who actually reads your resume in different scenarios.

Read the tutorial: [Build Your Resume with AI - Lesson 01](https://github.com/sanhehu/learn_build_resume-project/tree/01-Hiring)

## Actionable Items

1. Read the full tutorial from start to finish
2. Understand the three distinct hiring scenarios (campus recruiting, direct applications, experienced hiring)
3. Identify the key players: Hiring Manager, Recruiter, and ATS
4. Reflect on which scenario applies to your current job search

**Estimated time:** 15-20 minutes

## Checklist

- [ ] **Read the tutorial** - Complete reading of all sections in README.md
- [ ] **Understand the three scenarios** - Can explain the difference between campus recruiting, direct applications, and experienced hiring
- [ ] **Know the key players** - Can describe what Hiring Manager, Recruiter, and ATS do
- [ ] **Personal reflection** - Identified which hiring scenario is most relevant to your situation

## Submission & Verification

This is a reading-only lesson with no code or deliverables. When you feel confident about the concepts, proceed to the next lesson.

## Grading Rubric

> **For instructors and /teach-check assistant** — Students may skip this section.

- **Comprehension check:** Ask the student to explain which hiring scenario they fall into and why
- **Concept verification:** Student can differentiate between the roles of Hiring Manager, Recruiter, and ATS
- **Application:** Student can articulate how this knowledge will influence their resume strategy
