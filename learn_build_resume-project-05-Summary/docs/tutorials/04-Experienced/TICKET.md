# Experienced Hire Resume Writing (1-5 Years)

## Objective

Learn how to write a resume that showcases Impact, not just job duties. Understand how experienced resumes differ from new grad resumes, master the art of quantifying achievements, and learn to tailor for different roles.

Read the tutorial: [Build Your Resume with AI - Lesson 04](https://github.com/easyscale-academy/learn_build_resume-project/tree/04-Experienced/)

## Actionable Items

1. Read the full tutorial from start to finish
2. Understand what changes when you have experience (Work Experience becomes core, Education drops to bottom)
3. Learn the Action + Technical Detail + Measurable Impact formula for work experience
4. Study how to find and present your impact numbers (internal and external sources)
5. Understand tailoring: same experience, different emphasis for different roles
6. Review common mistakes and identify any you might be making

**Estimated time:** 25-30 minutes

## Checklist

- [ ] **Understand structural changes** - Know why Work Experience moves to the center and Education drops to the bottom
- [ ] **Summary for experienced hires** - Can write a Summary with identity, skills, and standout achievement
- [ ] **Impact over duties** - Understand the difference between listing job duties vs showing measurable results
- [ ] **Finding impact numbers** - Know internal sources (Manager, PM, dashboards) and external sources (press releases, public data)
- [ ] **Tailoring concept** - Understand how to reframe the same experience for different target roles
- [ ] **Common mistakes awareness** - Can identify the 6 major mistakes (duties without impact, no quantification, tech dumping, etc.)

## Submission & Verification

This is a reading-only lesson with no code or deliverables. When you feel confident about the concepts, proceed to apply them to your own resume.

## Grading Rubric

- **Structural understanding:** Student can explain why experienced resumes prioritize Work Experience over Projects and Education
- **Impact articulation:** Student can differentiate between job duty descriptions and impact-focused bullet points
- **Quantification skills:** Student knows multiple sources for finding impact numbers and understands confidentiality considerations
- **Tailoring application:** Student can explain how to reframe the same job experience for Backend vs Data vs ML roles
- **Career growth display:** Student understands how to show promotions and scope expansion on a resume
