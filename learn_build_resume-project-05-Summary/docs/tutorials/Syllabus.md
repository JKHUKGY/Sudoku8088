# Syllabus

Learn to build a competitive resume using AI tools. This series covers the American hiring process, ATS systems, resume writing strategies for different career stages, and hands-on practice with AI-powered resume optimization. By the end, you'll have a polished, tailored resume ready for your job search.

## 01-Hiring

Understand how American companies hire before writing your resume. This lesson covers three hiring scenarios: big company campus recruiting, small/mid-size direct applications, and experienced hiring. Learn who reads your resume (Hiring Manager, Recruiter, ATS) and how each scenario requires a different strategy. Foundation for all resume decisions ahead.

## 02-ATS

Debunk ATS myths and understand how resume screening actually works. Learn that ATS is a database for tracking—not an AI that rejects you. Expose five common misconceptions (keyword stuffing, white font tricks, fake scores). Understand AI-era changes: application volume explosion, AI-assisted sorting, platform behavior signals. Core principle: write for humans, not systems.

## 03-New-Grad

Building on hiring process and ATS knowledge, learn practical new grad resume writing. Master resume structure (Summary, Education, Skills, Projects), understand why you need multiple resume versions for different job directions, and apply the Action + Technical Detail + Impact formula. Avoid common mistakes like tech overload and homework-as-projects. Projects are your proof of capability.

## 04-Experienced

Transition from new grad to experienced hire resumes (1-5 years). Work Experience becomes the core; Education drops to the bottom. Learn to write Impact, not job duties—quantify achievements with real business data. Master techniques for finding impact numbers from internal and external sources. Tailor same experience differently for Backend vs Data vs ML roles. Series theory complete.

## 05-Summary

Hands-on practice: use AI to write resume Summaries. Master `/summary-writer` for interactive drafting and `/summary-reviewer` for harsh interviewer feedback. Learn the two-window workflow—one writes, one challenges—iterating 2-3 rounds until polished. Prepare 3 Summaries with different positioning angles (startup, product-focused, AI-native, data-driven). Same person, multiple directions. Theory meets practice.
